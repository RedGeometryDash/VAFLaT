About VAFLaT

Visual Addon for Language Transfer

VAFLaT (pronounced V.A. flat) stands for Visual Addon for Language Transfer. Language Transfer is completely audio-based which is fine for many but others also need a visual component to interact with in order to learn optimally.

VAFLaT attempts to add that visual component without implementing memorization.

Question types

Multiple Choice

Each fill in the blank question has 4 questions with 1-2 answers. Some answers are meant to trick you so look out!

Fill in the Blank

Each fill in the blank question is meant to test your ability to form sentences. In some of the later lessons multiple answers were also implemented!

Card Matching

Each card matching question has 2 correct matching cards out of 4 total.

VAFLaT is a school project and is NOT an official Language Transfer product. We only use Language Transfer's audio.
